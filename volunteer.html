Volunteer for Seal Husbandry

Seal Husbandry

involves the comprehensive care, feeding, enrichment, habitat management, veterinary support, and training of seals in captivity (zoos, aquariums, rehab centers), focusing on their physical and behavioral well-being, often preparing them for release or ensuring long-term health. Key aspects include diet (fish, squid), enrichment (fake kelp, feeding boxes), regular health checks, clean water/environments, and specialized training for medical cooperation.
Fill out and submit a volunteer application through our contact before December 12, 2025, to be considered for our next New Volunteer Training. (Please note only adults 18+ who are no longer in high school are eligible to apply.) When contacted, complete a phone interview and pass a background check.
